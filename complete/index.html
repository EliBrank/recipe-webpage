Chocolate Chip Banana Muffins

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Step 1:

    Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, & salt in a bowl and set aside.

  2. Step 2:

    Stir bananas, milk, cinnamon, & vanilla in a second bowl and set aside.

  3. Step 3:

    Beat butter and sugars together. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each

  4. Step 4:

    Stir banana mixture into butter mixture.

  5. Step 5:

    Stir in dry ingredients until just blended.

  6. Step 6:

    Lightly fold in chocolate chips (do not overmix).

  7. Step 7:

    Pour batter into a greased muffin pan and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.

  8. Step 8:

    Bake at 325 degrees Fahrenheit for 22-25 minutes, or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 0.5 cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 0.5 cups white sugar
  • 0.5 cups brown sugar
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 Tbsp. milk
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
